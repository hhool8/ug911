Idle games, also known as clicker or incremental games, are video games where progress is made through minimal player interaction. Players typically earn resources automatically, which can be used to unlock upgrades or accelerate progress. Popular examples include Cookie Clicker and Idle Miner Tycoon. Known for their relaxing gameplay and sense of progression, idle games are perfect for casual play.
