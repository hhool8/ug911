Stickman games are simple, often cartoonish video games featuring stick-figure characters. These games span various genres, including action, fighting, platformers, and puzzle games. Examples include Stick Fight: The Game and Ragdoll Master. Known for their minimalist design and straightforward gameplay, stickman games are easy to pick up and often offer fast-paced and fun challenges.
