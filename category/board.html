Board games are a category of tabletop games that involve players moving pieces or tokens on a pre-marked surface or “board,” following a set of rules to achieve specific objectives. They often focus on strategy, luck, or a mix of both, with gameplay ranging from simple dice rolls to complex decision-making and planning. Players typically compete or collaborate to reach goals such as accumulating points, conquering territories, or solving puzzles. Board games come in a wide variety of themes and genres, from classic titles like chess and Monopoly to modern, narrative-driven games. They offer a social, face-to-face gaming experience, promoting interaction and engagement among players.
